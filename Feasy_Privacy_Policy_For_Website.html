Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Data We Collect

  • Business information (name, industry, financial data)
  • User profile information (name, email)
  • Financial forecasts and projections
  • Revenue streams and expense data
  • Authentication data (provider IDs, email addresses)
  • Subscription and payment status
  • Feedback and support communications (including device and app version when you submit feedback)
  • Photo attachments (when you choose to attach them)

We do not use third-party analytics or track your in-app behavior.

Authentication & Third-Party Services

We offer multiple sign-in options:

  • Google Sign-In: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile information from Google. Google's Privacy Policy applies to data collected during authentication.
  • Apple Sign-In: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your name and email (or private relay email). Apple's Privacy Policy applies to data collected during authentication.
  • Email/Password: Your password is securely encrypted and never stored in plain text.

We do not share your authentication data with third parties except as necessary to provide the service (e.g., syncing via CloudKit).

Data Export Functionality

Our app allows you to export your financial data in various formats (PDF, Excel, CSV). When you use export features:

  • Only data you explicitly choose to export is included
  • Exported files are created locally on your device
  • You control where and how exported files are shared
  • Export data may be encrypted for security
  • We do not automatically upload or share your exported data

Export Compliance: This app uses encryption technology for data protection. Export of encrypted data may be subject to export administration regulations.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide financial forecasting and reporting services
  • To generate business plans and financial projections
  • To improve app functionality and user experience
  • To provide customer support when requested
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Storage & Security

  • Data is stored locally on your device using Core Data
  • Local backups are encrypted for security
  • Optional CloudKit sync via your iCloud account (when enabled by you)
  • CloudKit data is stored in Apple's secure cloud infrastructure
  • CloudKit syncs your business data across your devices signed into the same iCloud account
  • Apple's CloudKit Privacy Policy applies to synced data
  • We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures
  • Data is not shared with third parties without your consent
  • Push notifications are delivered through Apple Push Notification Service (APNs)

Subscription & Payment Information

  • Subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store
  • We receive subscription status (Starter or Professional AI plan)
  • We do not receive or store your payment card details
  • Payment information is handled exclusively by Apple
  • Apple's App Store Privacy Policy applies to payment data
  • We use subscription status to enable/disable features

Analytics & Device Information

We do not use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs in the app.

Device and app information (e.g. device model, iOS version, app version) may be included only when you voluntarily submit feedback or contact support. We use this to help troubleshoot issues. We do not collect feature usage statistics, error logs, or performance metrics from the app.

Data Retention & Deletion

  • Business and financial data: We retain your data for as long as your account is active. We may introduce automatic deletion after a retention period (e.g. 5 years) in a future update; you will be notified if this changes.
  • Local backups: We may introduce backup retention (e.g. 90 days) in a future update; you will be notified if this applies.
  • CloudKit synced data: Retained until you delete your account or disable sync
  • Account data: Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request (typically immediately)
  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through app settings
  • After account deletion, data cannot be recovered
  • CloudKit data is removed from all your synced devices after deletion

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data at any time through the app
  • Export your data in standard formats (PDF, Excel, CSV, Word)
  • Delete your data and account through app settings
  • Control data sharing and export preferences
  • Request data correction or modification
  • Disable CloudKit sync at any time
  • Revoke photo library access in iOS Settings
